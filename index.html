Client Problem

A system-level risk involving security, operations, and social licence

System-Level Risk

Kumba faces a complex challenge where illegal mining and asset theft reduce operational efficiency, threaten worker safety, and disrupt production systems across the mining value chain.

Security vs Social License

Traditional security responses risk damaging community trust, as increased surveillance may be perceived as criminalising local communities rather than addressing underlying socio-economic issues.

Integrated Challenge

The issue extends beyond physical security — it is driven by unemployment, organised crime networks, and weak stakeholder relationships, requiring a governance-aligned and socially aware solution.

Core Problem Statement

Kumba Iron Ore faces a systemic risk where illegal mining and theft activities threaten worker safety, disrupt mining operations, increase security costs, and strain relationships with surrounding communities — ultimately placing its social licence to operate at risk.

Employees +

Employees operate in high-risk environments where illegal mining, theft, and organised syndicates create safety concerns.

Increased surveillance and security controls can improve safety, but may also create perceptions of mistrust if not handled correctly.

Key Needs: Safety, trust, clarity in communication, and fair treatment.

Opportunity: Position employees as partners in security through reporting tools, training, and inclusion in decision-making.

Communities +

Communities surrounding mining operations often experience limited economic benefit, which drives participation in illegal mining.

Security measures are sometimes perceived as exclusionary, increasing tension between mining companies and communities.

Key Needs: Inclusion, economic opportunity, and meaningful engagement.

Opportunity: Use platforms like Kumba Connect to enable community reporting, communication, and participation in protecting operations.

Government & Regulators +

Government stakeholders are responsible for enforcing regulation, ensuring compliance, and maintaining economic stability.

Illegal mining undermines tax revenue, safety standards, and national resource governance.

Key Needs: Compliance, transparency, and collaboration.

Opportunity: Align solutions with ESG frameworks and regulatory objectives, strengthening public-private partnerships.

Investors +

Investors focus on long-term profitability, risk exposure, and ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) performance.

Illegal mining and security failures increase operational risk, impacting financial returns and company reputation.

Key Needs: Risk mitigation, transparency, and sustainable performance.

Opportunity: Demonstrate measurable impact through data-driven security and stakeholder engagement strategies.

Our Value Proposition

We deliver integrated security solutions that reduce operational risk, strengthen community trust, and ensure governance compliance — protecting both assets and social licence to operate

Operational Value

  • Reduce illegal mining, theft, and infrastructure damage that disrupt production
  • Improve real-time visibility of risks to support faster decision-making
  • Ensure operational continuity across mines, logistics, and processing systems
